About

My name is Keegan Lynch and I was born and raised in Kirkland, Washington. I have enjoyed art and design from a young age which has allowed me to explore many different fields of design. During middleschool and highschool I spent a lot of time in the yearbook club where I learned that I also have a passion for layout and design. I also realized that some of my best ideas have come out of collaboration with my peers

View My Works

During my time at the University of Colorado in Boulder I was able to refine my skills and explore many new areas of design. I enjoyed connenting and learning from my professors, many of which are highly respected in the design world. I also enjoyed being around many other students who shared my passion for design.

Since graduating college I have done quite a bit of traveling including a recent trek to Everest base camp. I am now ready to find a entry-level design job where I can begin my career in design. I am looking for a company that is big on collaboration and will be a good environment for me to learn and grow.

Outside of design, I enjoy spending time outdoors hiking, snowboarding, and golfing. I also enjoy spending time with my friends and family. I am a bit of movie buff and I love to play videogames.
